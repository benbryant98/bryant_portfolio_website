About Me

My full name is Benjamin Curtis Bryant, middle name courtesy of my father. I recently married my best friend and together we have 3 adorable cats. As someone who grew up toying with an operating system quite literally as old as I was, technology has seemed ever present in my life. Similarly has my fascination and amazement with it, starting with basic flash games and windows paint, to learning how to make a choose-your-own-adventure game in Python, to present day where I hope to use my aptitude towards computers as a stepping stone to a career in development. Outside of computers, I am passionate about animals, enjoy the nerdier hobbies in life such as Magic the Gathering and Dungeons & Dragons, and getting obsessed with a random hobby every time the ADHD kicks in.
